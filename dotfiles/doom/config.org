** Org Auto-Tangle (literate config — from SystemCrafters)
Auto-tangle config.org to config.el on save:
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(defun my/org-babel-tangle-config ()
  "Tangle config.org if it's in doom config directory."
  (when (string-equal (file-name-nondirectory (buffer-file-name))
                      "config.org")
    (let ((org-confirm-babel-evaluate nil))
      (org-babel-tangle))))

(add-hook 'org-mode-hook
          (lambda () (add-hook 'after-save-hook #'my/org-babel-tangle-config nil t)))
#+end_src
